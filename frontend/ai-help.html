How do I present?

Click the "Screen Share" icon in your meeting bar. Choose whether to share your entire screen, a window, or a specific tab.

Can others see my notifications?

If you share only a "Window" or a "Tab", participants will not see notifications or other parts of your desktop.

Is the chat secure?

Yes, all messages in Archat rooms are end-to-end encrypted. No third parties can read your conversations.

The chat input has a built-in emoji picker. You can also use the Ctrl+E shortcut to open it quickly.
